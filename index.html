    Who We Are?

    A Community Built on Hope

    Team Uraan is a grassroots NGO based in Burdwan, West Bengal, dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable support. We believe every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and care.

    Founded by passionate volunteers, our work spans food relief, health camps, and educational programs — making a real, lasting difference in the lives of children and families who need it most.

    🎯 Our Mission

    To empower communities through compassionate, consistent humanitarian action.

    🔭 Our Vision

    A society where no child goes hungry, uneducated, or unheard.

    What We Do?

    Our Programs & Initiatives

    Every program we run is designed with love, powered by volunteers, and guided by the needs of the community.

    📚

    Education Support

    Free tutoring, books, and school supplies for underprivileged children to help them stay in school and thrive.

    🍛

    Food Distribution

    Regular meal drives and ration support ensuring no family in our community goes to bed hungry.

    🏥

    Health Camps

    Free medical check-ups, medicines, and health awareness camps bringing quality care to doorsteps.

    🩸

    Blood Donation Drives

    Organizing life-saving blood donation camps, connecting donors with patients in critical need across hospitals.
